About Us

We are a Trademark for audio visual art, working through the collaboration of creatives to optimise production and packaging of top quality audio-visual art.

Our goal is to build a community that fully trusts in our process so much so that our label on any project guarantees them of a great listening and watching experience.

For the Creatives

We understand what it means to be a modern day artist in the real and digital world.
That being said, it is our primary goal that through the support, collaboration and supervision within our team, We enable the growth of all creatives and artists under our brand.

We have a symbiotic relationship between the team and the brand because we share a common goal and that is the production of high quality art, and art is what we sell not the artist.
We believe strongly that the art should grow the artist and that is our priority, Growth.
